What Is Metaverse

The metaverse is a word used to describe the next generation of the internet. But it’s not a place.It’s direction and it’s already begun.The met averse is a digital world where you can meet friends, own things, work and play, and live online.It’s a digital collection of symbols and language that will over time use every sensory input the human mind can comprehend. From the beginning of the Internet, when blogs and forums began, communities formed around subjects that had never exit before. People lived new experience because of this invented from of communication. Just as they did, when the printing press came into existence.These were all step towards the idea of the met averse. The extension of the human mind to new ways of creating experience. And we will take it farther than you can imagine, but it’s not a new event. There will be no invented met averse. There will be only be a continued evolution. Because the met averse began, the day the first cave paintings were made.
